FORGET ALL OF THE STANDARD RULES AND CONVENTIONS:

Salesmanship is not about tired gimmicks, cliches or manipulation. First and foremost, it is a matter of inspiration.

To be truly successful, the salesperson must be inspired to achieve a higher calling. To believe that what they are selling is not simply easy or cool or convenient, but that it moves other people to take action that will benefit them.

When a Higher Sale's Founder Larry Coco experienced the tragic death of his daughter in 2002, he had to make the highest "sale" any one ever has to make: how to go on in life without his beloved child. To do so, Coco found the all-important inspiration: to "be Danielle’s legacy in this world.”
